|----------|---------|-----------------|---------------|

| `existing` | Current conditions | 2nd-6th photos | Photos 2-6 |

## Key UX Principles

1. **Zero Configuration** - Photos are auto-categorized, no setup needed
2. **Silent Learning** - System gets smarter without bothering user
3. **Construction Smart** - Knows what's needed for each job type
4. **Non-Intrusive** - No pop-ups, no suggestion cards, just works
5. **Progressive Enhancement** - New users get smart defaults, experienced users get personalization
6. **Mobile-First** - Capture on-site, refine in office
7. **Professional Output** - Photos enhance trust without overwhelming
